  • Come build
    with us.

    MakeHarvard was founded upon a belief of the importance of a collaborative maker experience. We’re encouraging students to turn concepts into reality by providing them with a platform to envision their new ideas within teams and model a cohesive product using Computer Aided Design software – to turn engineering design platforms into a canvas for their wildest ideas.




  • Frequently Asked Questions


    How does MakeHarvard work?

    Over a 24 hour period, teams of up to FIVE students will have the opportunity to create anything their hearts desire using our 3D printers, laser cutters, and much much more!

    Who is eligible to attend?

    Any undergraduate or graduate student enrolled in a degree-granting community college or university program over the age of 18 is eligible to attend.

    Are travel reimbursements available?

    We are unable to provide travel reimbursements to makers attending our event.

    What hardware will be available?

    Our exact hardware inventory will be finalized soon; expect access to many types of rapid-prototyping equipment!

    I have no experience as a Maker, can I still attend MakeHarvard?

    Of course! We do not filter our applications based on prior experience. Our team will look at applications on an individual basis and make admission decisions hollistically. Think of MakeHarvard as an opportunity to meet people of all engineering experience levels!

    Do I need a team?

    You don't need a team formed beforehand to participate in MakeHarvard. "Pitch Pit" ideation sessions will be held after the opening ceremony to help people find teams to work on their projects with.

    Other questions?

    Don't hesitate to reach out to us at makeharvard@gmail.com with any questions!
