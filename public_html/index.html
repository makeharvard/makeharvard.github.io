About



The annual MakeHarvard engineering makeathon will return this Winter for it's second year. Competing teams at our 2019 event will have access to rapid-prototyping equipment like 3D printers and laser cutters to produce engineering prototypes and compete for prizes.

Applications are open now! Apply via our Typeform by 11:59pm on Fri 11/30 to be considered.

Please email makeharvard@gmail.com with any questions.
